# UT1005 - Confront

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may directly confront the source about their suspicious behavior. This technique involves addressing the issue head-on, expressing concerns, and seeking an explanation from the source. While this approach carries the risk of escalating tensions, it can be effective in clarifying misunderstandings or deterring the source from engaging in further suspicious activities.
