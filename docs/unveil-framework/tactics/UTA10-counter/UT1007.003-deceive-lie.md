# UT1007.003 - Deceive: Lie

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may choose to lie in response to the source's suspicious behavior. This deceptive tactic can be used to protect sensitive information, mislead the source, or aid to maintain the control the direction of the conversation. While lying carries ethical and operational risks, it can be a necessary measure in situations where the truth could compromise the operation.
