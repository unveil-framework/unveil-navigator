# UT1011 - Move to safe topic

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may strategically redirect the conversation to a safer or less sensitive topic to maintain control over the interaction. This tactic involves avoiding potential triggers or contentious subjects that could disrupt the flow of the conversation or jeopardize the relationship with the source. By steering the discussion towards neutral or non-threatening topics, the handler can mitigate the risk of confrontation, resistance or disclosure of sensitive information. This technique allows the handler to maintain a sense of control and direction in the conversation while minimizing potential disruptions or obstacles.
