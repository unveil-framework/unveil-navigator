# UT1002 - Record

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may act normally and show no immediate reaction to suspicious behavior exhibited by the source, instead quietly documenting and archiving the information. This technique allows the handler to maintain the flow of the interaction without alerting the source to any concerns. By saving the information for later analysis, the handler can build a comprehensive picture of the source's behavior over time, supporting more informed decisions in future interactions.
