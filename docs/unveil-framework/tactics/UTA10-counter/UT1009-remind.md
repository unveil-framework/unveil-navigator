# UT1009 - Remind

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may remind the source of previous agreements, understandings or the potential consequences of their actions. This technique involves subtly or explicitly referencing past conversations, commitments or established norms to coerce the source into compliance or deter them from continuing suspicious behavior. By invoking reminders, the handler reinforces the operational framework and expectations.
