# UT1012 - Leave the interaction

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may choose to terminate the interaction abruptly in response to the source's suspicious behavior. This technique involves exiting the conversation or physical location to prevent further exposure or risk. Leaving the interaction can serve as a protective measure, allowing the handler to regroup, reassess the situation and plan a more strategic response.
