# UT1010 - Change the situation

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may change the physical or contextual setting of the interaction to disrupt the source's suspicious activities. This could involve relocating the meeting, altering the communication medium or introducing new environmental variables. Changing the situation can disorient the source and provide the handler with an opportunity to regain control and reassess the interaction.
