# UT1013 - Conduct counterintelligence checks

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may conduct counterintelligence checks once a suspicious indicator has been identified to subsequently mitigate any potential infiltration or influence attempts by adversarial entities. This involves verifying the background, associations and behavior of the source, looking for any anomalies or inconsistencies that might indicate subversive activities. By performing these checks, the handler can detect and respond to potential threats, ensuring that the source remains trustworthy and that the operation remains secure.
