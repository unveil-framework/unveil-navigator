# UT1007 - Deceive

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may employ deception as a defensive measure when indicators suggest that the source might be attempting to manipulate, influence or extract information from the handler. This technique involves presenting false or misleading information to the source, as well as hiding or faking the handler’s emotions and gestures to obscure their true intentions, capabilities or operational details. This tactic must be used judiciously and with careful consideration of potential long-term consequences on the handler-source relationship and the operation's overall success.
