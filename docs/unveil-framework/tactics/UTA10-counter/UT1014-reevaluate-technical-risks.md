# UT1014 - Reevaluate technical risks

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may review and enhance technical safety measures when it becomes apparent that the source or adversary is more proficient at technology and cyberattacks than initially anticipated. This technique involves conducting a thorough assessment of existing security protocols, such as encrypted communications, secure file storage, and access controls, to identify potential vulnerabilities. The handler will then implement additional or upgraded measures, including advanced encryption practices, multi-factor authentication and sophisticated counter-surveillance techniques, to fortify the operation’s defenses. By proactively adapting to the heightened technical capabilities of the source or adversary, the handler ensures the continued protection of communication channels, data integrity and overall operational security.
