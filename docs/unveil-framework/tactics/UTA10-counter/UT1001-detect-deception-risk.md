# UT1001 - Detect deception risk

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may try to detect the level of deception risk of the source. In order to do this, the handler will initially establish a baseline of the normal behavioral indicators of truthfulness of the source, which will later be used to detect deviations from in that may indicate a higher risk of deception. It must be noted that the detection of one deceit indicator or baseline deviation does not necessarily imply that the source is lying. In order to consider that the deceit risk has increased, multiple indicators (a cluster) must be detected and, even then, it does not mean that the source is necessarily lying since there might be other environmental circumstances that may trigger these indicators such as thinking something through (not necessarily trying to lie), amongst others. The deception detection risk encompasses the following phases: establish baseline, create and enforce a detection plan and detect deviation indicators.
