# UT1007.004 - Deceive: Fake emotion or reaction

**Tactic:** [UTA10 - Counter](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may feign an emotional response or reaction to the source's behavior. This technique involves displaying a contrived emotional state, such as surprise, concern, or indifference, to influence the source's perceptions and behavior. By controlling their emotional display, the handler can guide the interaction in a desired direction and potentially elicit useful information from the source.
