# UT0301.010 - Profile the possible sources: Cultural factors

**Tactic:** [UTA03 - Screening](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may find out the cultural factors that influence the source's way of thinking and behaving. For instance, this may involve a review of how the source frequently posts about local festivals, religious events and national holidays. These posts include personal anecdotes, photos and even traditional recipes, indicating a strong cultural identity. Thus, the handler may notice that the source often shares content related to regional political movements and expresses pride in their cultural heritage.
