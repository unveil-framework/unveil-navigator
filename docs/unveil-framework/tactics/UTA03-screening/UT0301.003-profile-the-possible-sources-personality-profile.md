# UT0301.003 - Profile the possible sources: Personality profile

**Tactic:** [UTA03 - Screening](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may find out the source's personality profile. The recommended model to be followed by the handler is the PEN model,which includes the traits of psychoticism, extraversion and neuroticism. The profiling of the source may be done by the collection of indirect indicators of its traits, obtained by the analysis of documents, questioning people who they interact with or by the observation of the source's behavior in social media and real life. Profiles are considered live documents, as they are to be updated by screeners and handlers. They can also be translated into other equivalent personality models, such as the Big Five model, depending on the needs of the operation and the abilities and knowledge of the handler and screeners.
