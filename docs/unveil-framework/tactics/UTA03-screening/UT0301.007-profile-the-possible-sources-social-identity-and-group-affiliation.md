# UT0301.007 - Profile the possible sources: Social identity and group affiliation

**Tactic:** [UTA03 - Screening](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify the source's social identity, an individual's self-concept that is derived from their membership in various social groups82. Therefore, the handler may identify the belonging and reference groups of the source, which include: ethnic background, religion, gender, sex, nationality, sexual orientation and any other group relevant to the source. Understanding the source's social identity will not only help the handler understand the groups they belong to, but also the ones they want to be a part of, as well as their attitude towards them. This can, in turn, help the handler tailor the approach and the subsequent persuasion techniques.
