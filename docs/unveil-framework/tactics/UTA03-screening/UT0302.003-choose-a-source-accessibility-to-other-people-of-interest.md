# UT0302.003 - Choose a source: Accessibility to other people of interest

**Tactic:** [UTA03 - Screening](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may evaluate the source's current or potential access to the people of interest to the operation. This might mean using the initial source as an intermediate step necessary in order to approach another source. UT0302.004 Choose a source: Susceptibility to persuasion The handler may evaluate the source's level of susceptibility to persuasion. This should be assessed by considering the source's personality and motivation profile, susceptibility to incentives, either rewards or punishments, and general vulnerabilities, as well as their level of security training. UT0302.005 Choose a source: Determine collaboration motivations This TTP may include but it is not limited to identify economic reasons, ideology or affiliation, affiliation, sexual interest or any other motivation that may boost or mine the motivation to collaborate with the handler.
