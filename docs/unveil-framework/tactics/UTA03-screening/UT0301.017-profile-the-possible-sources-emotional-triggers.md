# UT0301.017 - Profile the possible sources: Emotional triggers

**Tactic:** [UTA03 - Screening](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may find out the source's emotional triggers, that is, specific stimuli that generate certain emotions on the source. These triggers may be related to other factors included in the screening process and may be considered vulnerabilities. For instance, if the source frequently reacts strongly to discussions about economic hardships or social injustice, these topics can be identified as emotional triggers. The handler can then craft messages or engage in conversations that touch on these sensitive subjects to elicit emotional responses, building rapport and trust. This understanding of the source’s emotional landscape allows the handler to exploit these vulnerabilities, guiding the source towards revealing valuable information or taking specific actions aligned with the handler's objectives.
