# UT0608 - Establish a dominance status

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler establishes a relationship in which it is clear for the source that the one who will dominate the interactions and relationship is the handler. This may be done by adopting a position of power or authority over the source (boss, handler, expert in a relevant matter, higher rank, etc.). This technique is more effective with certain personality profiles, such as low psychoticism individuals, though it should be conducted with great care when dealing with high psychoticism sources126.
