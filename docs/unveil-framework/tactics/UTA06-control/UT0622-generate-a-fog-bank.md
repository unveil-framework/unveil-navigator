# UT0622 - Generate a fog bank

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may stop an argument with the source by creating fog banks. This technique involves telling the other person that they are right, which makes them stop arguing for a moment, allowing the handler to reorient the conversation.

The handler is trying to obtain the required information or access from the source in order to fulfill the operation's requirements. Impact techniques regarding the collection of information must not be confused with the information gathering of the screening phase, the first one being the collection of specific information required by the agency while the second refers to finding out general source areas of knowledge.
