# UT0603.001 - Approach the source: Third party introduction

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler leverages the existence of an influential figure trusted by the source to make the initial introduction. This method capitalizes on the pre-existing trust and rapport between the source and the third party, thereby reducing suspicion and resistance towards the handler. It's particularly effective in environments where direct approaches might be rebuffed or where the source maintains a cautious or closed social circle.
