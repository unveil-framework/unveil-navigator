# UT0621 - Use trusted third party

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may leverage the influence of a trusted third party to exert pressure on the source. This tactic involves identifying individuals who hold sway over the source, whether through familial, professional or personal connections. By subtly involving these third parties in the interaction, the handler can indirectly influence the source's behavior and decisions. This technique capitalizes on the source's existing relationships and social dynamics to reinforce compliance with the handler's objectives.
