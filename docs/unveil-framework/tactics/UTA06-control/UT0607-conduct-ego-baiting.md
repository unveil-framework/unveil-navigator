# UT0607 - Conduct ego-baiting

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may hurt the source's ego to incite them to respond or act in a specific way, usually out of pride or to defend their self-image. This method relies on challenging, questioning, or even undermining someone's capabilities, opinions, or achievements with the goal of eliciting an emotional response. By pricking the individual's ego, the aim is to provoke them into reacting to prove their worth or correct the perception that has been suggested about them. In the cyber domain, particularly where pseudo-anonymity is prevalent, this technique can compel targets to inadvertently disclose information about themselves in an effort to validate their claims or actions. Examples of effective triggers might include dismissing a claim as impossible or suggesting that the target lacks the necessary knowledge or skill to accomplish what they claim, prompting them to reveal more to prove their competence.
