# UT0603 - Approach the source

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may apply the selected approach technique or combination of techniques in order to initiate contact with the source, prior to establishing rapport. The chosen approach technique may be maintained throughout the relationship, adapted or changed depending on a variety of factors such as the source’s responsiveness, the operational environment, or any other intelligence requirements.
