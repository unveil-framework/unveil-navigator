# UT0614 - Establish a mutual benefit relationship

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may establish a relationship based on mutual benefit. This is especially useful when the source knows they are dealing with an intelligence handler. In this case, it is not feelings that are the focus of the handler's control over the source, but rather a mutual understanding of the usefulness of the relationship for both parts.
