# UT0617.004 - Gaslight: Create confusion by introducing contradictory information

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may introduce contradictory information or evidence to challenge the source's version of events, causing confusion and undermining their confidence in their own memory. By presenting conflicting narratives, the handler aims to distort the source's perception of reality and weaken their defenses, making them more vulnerable to subsequent manipulation.
