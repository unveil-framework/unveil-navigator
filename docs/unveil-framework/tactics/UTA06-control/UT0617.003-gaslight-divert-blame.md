# UT0617.003 - Gaslight: Divert blame

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may shift responsibility for past actions or events onto others, including the source, deflecting accountability away from themselves or their organization. By attributing blame to external factors or individuals, the handler seeks to distort the source's understanding of who is truly responsible, creating confusion and weakening their trust in their own judgment, making them more vulnerable to subsequent manipulation.
