# UT0617.001 - Gaslight: Deny

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may deny events or situations that the source remembers accurately, insisting that they never happened or were misconstrued. By repeatedly denying the validity of the source's memories, the handler aims to sow doubt and undermine the source's confidence in their own recollections, making them more vulnerable to subsequent manipulation.
