# UT0617.002 - Gaslight: Minimize

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may downplay the significance of the source's events or experiences, making them seem trivial or insignificant. Through minimizing the importance of the source's memories, the handler seeks to diminish their sense of reality and erode their trust in their own perceptions, making them more vulnerable to subsequent manipulation.
