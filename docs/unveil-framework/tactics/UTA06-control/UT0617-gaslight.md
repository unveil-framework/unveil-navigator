# UT0617 - Gaslight

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may gaslight the source in order to distort their judgment by manipulating them psychologically to distort their perception of reality and undermine their trust in their own memory, judgment and sanity. This may difficult the source's defenses and make them emotionally controllable by the handler. It must be noted that the usage of this technique must be subtle, while the potential adverse effects on rapport must be considered prior to using it.
