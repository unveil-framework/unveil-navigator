# UT0603.002 - Approach the source: Common groups

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler either joins or simulates membership in organizations, clubs, social groups, or online communities that the source is already a part of. By sharing common interests, goals, or enemies, the handler can naturally build rapport and gain the source's trust. This technique relies on the psychological principle of ”ingroup favoritism”, where individuals are more likely to trust and cooperate with others whom they consider part of their social group125.
