# UT0616 - Subvert against own network

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may subvert the source's own network, turning individuals within it into unwitting assets or informants for the handler's objectives. This can be accomplished by exploiting vulnerabilities or weaknesses within the source's network, such as leveraging personal relationships, compromising confidential information or creating dissent or conflict among members. By exerting influence over the source's network, the handler can further solidify their control over the source and the collection process.
