# UT0618.002 - Coerce: Threat to expose collaboration

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

Similarly, the handler may employ the threat of revealing the source's collaboration with intelligence operatives to coerce their continued cooperation. This tactic capitalizes on the source's desire to maintain secrecy and avoid repercussions for their involvement in clandestine activities. The handler may insinuate or directly threaten to disclose the source's role as an informant or asset, thereby compelling them to adhere to the handler's instructions and provide ongoing intelligence support.
