# UT0618.001 - Coerce: Threat to expose information

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may use the threat of exposing sensitive or compromising information about the source to coerce them into cooperation. This could include threatening to reveal personal secrets, past indiscretions, or illegal activities that could damage the source's reputation or livelihood. By leveraging the fear of exposure, the handler seeks to manipulate the source into complying with their demands and divulging valuable intelligence.
