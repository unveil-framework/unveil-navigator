# UT0603.006 - Approach the source: Reaction

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may react to a comment, post or story of the source on social media. This subtechnique does not refer to commenting or sending written responses to the source, but to the act of “reacting” with some kind of multimedia to what the source posted. For example, if the source posts a story on Instagram, the handler can react with a fire emoji to indicate that they like or feel attracted to that picture, without explicitly sending a message that says “hey, I think you look good in your photo”.
