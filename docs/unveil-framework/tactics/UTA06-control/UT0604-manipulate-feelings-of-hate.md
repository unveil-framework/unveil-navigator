# UT0604 - Manipulate feelings of hate

**Tactic:** [UTA06 - Control](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may link existing or newly created feelings of hate towards an object to a tangible manifestation of said emotion to manipulate the source into collaborating. This is done by offering the source a way in which they can express or relieve their hate feelings. In an online HUMINT context, a handler might exploit a source's animosity towards a corrupt government official by amplifying their feelings of hatred and frustration. The handler could leverage the source’s hate towards this official by presenting an opportunity for the source to anonymously contribute information that could expose the official, providing a tangible outlet for the source's emotions.
