# UT0509.001 - Peripheral key: Credibility by expertise

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may establish credibility by emphasizing their expertise or competence in relevant areas. By showcasing knowledge, qualifications or experience, the handler can enhance their perceived trustworthiness and persuasiveness.

Expertise can be real or fake, and it can be displayed in different ways: academic titles, verbal fluidity, technical language and contributions, display of data, specific outfit (like a white coat), etc.
