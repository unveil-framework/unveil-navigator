# UT0516 - Generate obedience

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may utilize authority to elicit obedience from the source. This technique involves presenting the source with authoritative commands or instructions, backed by the perceived authority of the handler or the context. By exploiting the source's inclination to comply with authority figures, the handler can influence the source's behavior and foster collaboration. However, it is crucial for the handler to ensure that ethical boundaries are respected and that the source's well-being is safeguarded throughout the process. This can be shown by either explicit or tacit obedience or by manifesting respect for superiority or hierarchy in any form. It must be noted that some personality profiles are more vulnerable to this technique 115 (low psychoticism), whereas using authority and trying to generate obedience with others (high psychoticism individuals, mainly) may generate reactance or be counterproductive.
