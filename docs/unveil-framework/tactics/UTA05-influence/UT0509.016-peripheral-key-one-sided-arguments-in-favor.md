# UT0509.016 - Peripheral key: One-sided arguments in favor

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may present one-sided arguments in favor of their position to enhance persuasion. Focusing exclusively on supporting evidence, benefits or advantages associated with the handler's message can bias the source's perception and decision-making in favor of compliance. Leveraging one-sided arguments as a peripheral key minimizes exposure to counterarguments or conflicting information, increasing the source's likelihood of accepting the handler's message.
