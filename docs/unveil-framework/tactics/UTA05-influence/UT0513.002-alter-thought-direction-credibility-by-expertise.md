# UT0513.002 - Alter thought direction: Credibility by expertise

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may bolster their credibility by showcasing their expertise, qualifications, or experience in relevant domains. By demonstrating competence and authority in the subject matter at hand, the handler seeks to persuade the source to place greater trust in their opinions, recommendations, and proposed course of action.
