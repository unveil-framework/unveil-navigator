# UT0501.009 - Tailor the message:Social identity

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to align with the source's social identity or group affiliation. By incorporating language, values or beliefs associated with the groups the source identifies with, the handler can enhance trust and rapport. This technique relies on the understanding of social psychology and group dynamics to effectively resonate with the source's sense of belonging and identity. In an online HUMINT context, a handler could apply this technique by engaging with a source who is an active member of a specific online community, such as a veterans' forum. In this case, by tailoring their messages to include military jargon, expressing shared values like loyalty and honor and referencing common experiences from, for example, the military service, the handler may align their communication with the source's social identity as a veteran. In the case of a technical community, a handler may be able to get identified with the source quoting past experiences and successes which may be useful to cultivate a deeper social link. 98
