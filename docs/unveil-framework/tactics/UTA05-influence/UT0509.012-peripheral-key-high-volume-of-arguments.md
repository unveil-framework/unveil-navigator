# UT0509.012 - Peripheral key: High volume of arguments

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may present a high volume of arguments or reasons to enhance persuasion. Providing multiple justifications, examples, or pieces of evidence in support of the handler's position increases their receptiveness to persuasion. Leveraging a high volume of arguments as a peripheral key strengthens the persuasiveness of the handler's message and diminishes counterarguments or doubts.
