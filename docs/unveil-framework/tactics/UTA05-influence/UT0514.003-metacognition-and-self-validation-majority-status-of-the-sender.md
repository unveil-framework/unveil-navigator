# UT0514.003 - Metacognition and self-validation: Majority status of the sender

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may strategically convey information or anecdotes that highlight the widespread acceptance or endorsement of the persuasive object or idea by a majority of individuals or groups. By emphasizing the consensus or popularity of the message, the handler aims to encourage the source to align their thoughts or attitudes with those of the majority, thus validating their beliefs.
