# UT0509.014 - Peripheral key: Explicit conclusions

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may provide explicit conclusions or recommendations to enhance persuasion. Clearly stating the desired outcome or course of action can guide the source's decision-making process and reduce ambiguity or uncertainty. Leveraging explicit conclusions as a peripheral key facilitates comprehension and acceptance of the handler's message, increasing the source's likelihood of compliance.
