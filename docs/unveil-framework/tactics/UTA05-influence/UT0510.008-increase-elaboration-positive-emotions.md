# UT0510.008 - Increase elaboration: Positive emotions

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler can evoke positive emotions in the source to increase their motivation to process information only if the receptor perceives that the positive emotion will not disappear once they elaborate. By creating an environment that induces feelings of happiness, optimism or excitement, handlers can enhance the source's engagement and openness to the information.
