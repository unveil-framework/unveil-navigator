# UT0510.001 - Increase elaboration: Low honesty

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may be or use someone else who is considered dishonest by the source in order to increase their elaboration. In other words, if the source is listening or reading what someone who they do not consider honest (for example, a member of a different political party or someone from a group considered an adversary or enemy) says, they will think more about the content of the message.
