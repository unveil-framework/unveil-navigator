# UT0504.004 - Use information probes: Changing conversation or situation conditions

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may change the conversation topic or the conditions of the interaction to probe for information. By introducing new subjects or altering the environment, the handler can observe how the source adapts and what information they volunteer. This technique is useful for assessing the source's flexibility, priorities and hidden knowledge, all while maintaining the handler's covert role.
