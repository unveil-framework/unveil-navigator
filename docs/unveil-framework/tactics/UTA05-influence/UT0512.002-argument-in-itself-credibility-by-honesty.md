# UT0512.002 - Argument in itself: Credibility by honesty

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may build credibility by being transparent and honest in their interactions with the source. This involves acknowledging limitations, admitting mistakes and providing truthful information even if it may be unfavorable. Honesty fosters trust between the handler and the source, increasing the likelihood of collaboration and persuasion. Honesty can be easily displayed by being coherent and impartial.
