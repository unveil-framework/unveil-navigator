# UT0514.005 - Metacognition and self-validation: Positive emotion

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may intentionally generate positive emotions on the handler at the end of their message in order to make them validate their previously formed thoughts about what the handler is talking about. For example, a handler might engage with a target discussing workplace challenges. Throughout the conversation, the handler subtly emphasizes the importance of teamwork and trust in overcoming obstacles. As the discussion wraps up, the handler shares an inspiring anecdote about a previous team project where mutual support led to success and recognition. This story is intended to evoke positive emotions in the target, such as pride and camaraderie, leading them to validate and align with the handler's earlier suggestions about the value of teamwork.
