# UT0509.003 - Peripheral key: Physical attractiveness

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may leverage physical attractiveness as a peripheral cue to enhance their persuasive impact on the source. Physical appearance, grooming, and demeanor can influence the source's perception of the handler's credibility, likability and trustworthiness by creating a halo effect.
