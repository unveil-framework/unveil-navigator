# UT0512.005 - Argument in itself: Losses approach

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may employ a losses approach by highlighting the negative aspects of not making a specific decision or action. By emphasizing what the source stands to lose by not cooperating, such as missed opportunities or risks to their own interests, the handler aims to motivate the source to take action.
