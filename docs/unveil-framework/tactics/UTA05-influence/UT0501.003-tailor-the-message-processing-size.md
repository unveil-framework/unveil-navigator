# UT0501.003 - Tailor the message:Processing size

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to the source's processing size, that is, whether they process information in big or small chunks. People who tend to process big chunks of information may be approached with a more holistic point of view, describing the whole of a situation, while people who tend to process information on smaller chunks should be approached with a detailed description of a situation91. If the designated source tends to have a more holistic way of seeing the world, the handler can send long, extensive text messages and deep reflections, whereas if the source processes information in small chunks, they could explain in detail a specific topic of information.
