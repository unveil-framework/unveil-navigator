# UT0501.005 - Tailor the message:Need for cognition

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to the source's need for cognition, the level to which a person thinks they enjoy thinking about a topic95, by presenting an idea or topic as attractive to people who take pleasure in pondering in order to make them choose that idea or topic, guiding them and giving them an intrinsic reward by satisfying said need96. For example, if a source has a clearly high need for cognition, the handler may mention how a certain topic they want the source to be interested in really made them ponder about something or how much they enjoyed learning about it.
