# UT0510.007 - Increase elaboration: Physical attractiveness

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may leverage their physical attractiveness to enhance the source's motivation to process information. Physical attractiveness can lead to positive first impressions. This technique may exploit the halo effect, where good-looking individuals are often attributed with a wide range of favorable traits, to elevate high elaboration processing of information. Although what is considered physically attractive varies greatly among cultures and even individuals, this technique can be generated even by just looking polished and clean.
