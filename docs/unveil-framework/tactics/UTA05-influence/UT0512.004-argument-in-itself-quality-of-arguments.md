# UT0512.004 - Argument in itself: Quality of arguments

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may persuade a source in high elaboration with high quality arguments by presenting logical reasoning, evidence, and examples to support their points. Strong, wellreasoned arguments are more persuasive and convincing to the source. According to scientific research, a high quality argument can be defined by their logical nature, as well as their truthfulness, causality, high occurrence probability and high deseability.
