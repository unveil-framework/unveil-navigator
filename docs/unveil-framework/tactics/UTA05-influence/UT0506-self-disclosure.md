# UT0506 - Self disclosure

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may disclose a fact or story about their persona to incite the source to provide them with similar information about themselves. It must be noted that research shows that self disclosure on its own does not ensure the obtention of the information the handler needs107, so it is advised to combine this technique with other Influence techniques. For example, the handler may pose as a fellow activist in a certain forum or social media, sharing personal stories about attending a recent protest, mentioning specific details and emotions they experienced. This self-disclosure aims to build rapport and trust, encouraging the source to reciprocate with their own experiences and feelings about similar activities that may give the handler the information they are looking for.
