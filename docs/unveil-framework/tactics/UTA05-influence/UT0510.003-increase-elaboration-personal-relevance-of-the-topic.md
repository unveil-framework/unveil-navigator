# UT0510.003 - Increase elaboration: Personal relevance of the topic

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor the conversation to emphasize the personal relevance of the topic under discussion to the source's own life experiences, values, or aspirations. By highlighting how the information or ideas being presented directly impact the source's interests or goals, the handler can increase the source's motivation to engage in elaborative processing.
