# UT0509.015 - Peripheral key: Recency effect

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may capitalize on the recency effect to enhance persuasion. This implies leaving the most effective arguments at the end of the message, as the source may remember them better. Leveraging the recency effect as a peripheral key prioritizes recent stimuli in the source's memory, increasing their likelihood of being influenced by recent persuasive messages.
