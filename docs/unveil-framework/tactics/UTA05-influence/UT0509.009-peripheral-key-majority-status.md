# UT0509.009 - Peripheral key: Majority status

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may emphasize the consensus, showing that the handler’s opinion is shared by a majority, to enhance persuasion. Highlighting the widespread acceptance or endorsement of a particular idea, product or course of action can influence the source's perception of its validity and desirability. Leveraging sender majority status as a peripheral key encourages the source to conform to prevailing norms or opinions, increasing their receptiveness to persuasion.
