# UT0514.006 - Metacognition and self-validation: Matching with self-monitoring

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their persuasive message to align with the source's self-monitoring tendencies or personality traits. By presenting arguments or appeals that resonate with the source's self-concept or identity, the handler aims to increase the source's sense of relevance and personal connection to the message. This matching process enhances the source's perception of the message's validity and increases their likelihood of self-validation.
