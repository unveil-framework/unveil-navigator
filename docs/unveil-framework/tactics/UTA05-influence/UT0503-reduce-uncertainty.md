# UT0503 - Reduce uncertainty

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may reduce the source’s uncertainty about a topic that bothers or interests them so that they develop a higher feeling of trust towards the handler. This technique may especially be useful in highly neurotic sources when applied jointly with the persuasion principle of social proof106, as they tend to feel more anxiety in every situation. For example, a handler could apply this technique by engaging with a highly neurotic source who is anxious about the safety of their financial investments. The handler might start by sharing credible and reassuring information about market stability, thus reducing the source's uncertainty.
