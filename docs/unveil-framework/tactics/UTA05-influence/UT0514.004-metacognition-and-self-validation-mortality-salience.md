# UT0514.004 - Metacognition and self-validation: Mortality salience

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may subtly evoke thoughts or reminders of mortality in the source's mind. By highlighting the transient nature of life or the importance of making meaningful connections and contributions, the handler aims to stimulate introspection and self-reflection in the source. To invoke mortality salience, a handler might end the conversation with a source subtly steering the discussion towards topics like natural disasters, pandemics or notable figures' deaths. For example, during a chat about a recent high-profile natural disaster, the handler might comment on the unpredictability of life and the importance of leaving a legacy.
