# UT0514.001 - Metacognition and self-validation: Credibility by expertise

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may strategically emphasize their expertise or knowledge in relevant domains to enhance their credibility in the eyes of the source. By presenting themselves as knowledgeable or skilled in areas related to the topic of discussion, the handler aims to increase the source's trust and validation of their thoughts on the proposal presented by the handler.
