# UT0501.010 - Tailor the message:Morality

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to appeal to the source's moral values and principles. By framing information or requests in a way that aligns with the source's moral compass, the handler can foster a sense of moral alignment and trust. For example, if, by researching the source’s social media activity, the handler identifies the source's strong advocacy for climate change action, they can emphasize how sharing specific information could help combat environmental degradation, presenting the request as a moral duty to protect the planet.
