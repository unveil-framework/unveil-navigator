# UT0505 - Reduce perceived risk

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may reduce the source's perceived risk about a topic that bothers or interests them so that they develop a higher feeling of trust towards the handler. However, it must be noted that certain personality profiles, like those with a very high level of psychoticism, may be more drawn towards options that imply a higher risk, so this technique may be counterproductive. A handler might engage with a source on a social media platform where the source has expressed concerns about privacy or data security. By empathetically acknowledging these concerns and sharing reassurances about robust data protection measures and confidentiality protocols, the handler can alleviate the source's fears. For instance, if the source is worried about their personal information being exposed, the handler could explain the stringent measures in place to safeguard their identity and ensure their data is secure offering the usage of robust cryptography.
