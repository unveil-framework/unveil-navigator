# UT0513.001 - Alter thought direction: Majority status of the sender

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may strategically highlight the fact that a significant number of individuals or groups, particularly respected or influential ones, share the same viewpoint or have taken similar actions as the handler. For example, say a handler engaging with a potential source on a forum about cybersecurity might strategically emphasize that many respected professionals and experts in the industry advocate for the adoption of a particular security protocol. By citing influential figures and organizations, such as renowned cybersecurity firms, government agencies and well-known industry leaders who have endorsed this protocol, the handler can leverage social proof to build credibility and persuade the source to align with this viewpoint.
