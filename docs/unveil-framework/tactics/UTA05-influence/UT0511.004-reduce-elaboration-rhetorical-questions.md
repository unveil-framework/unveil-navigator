# UT0511.004 - Reduce elaboration: Rhetorical questions

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may use rhetorical questions, if the receiver is highly motivated, to reduce their elaboration. Rhetorical questions can bypass critical thinking processes and encourage the receiver to accept the implied answer without further analysis, especially when they are already inclined to agree with the handler's perspective.
