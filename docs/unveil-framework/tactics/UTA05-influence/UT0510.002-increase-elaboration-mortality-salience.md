# UT0510.002 - Increase elaboration: Mortality salience

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may subtly introduce themes or discussions related to mortality, existential concerns, or the fragility of life during interactions with the source. By invoking mortality salience, the handler can stimulate deep cognitive processing and reflection in the source, leading to heightened elaboration on relevant topics. To invoke mortality salience, a handler might engage a source in a casual conversation about current events, subtly steering the discussion towards topics like natural disasters, pandemics, or notable figures' deaths. For instance, during a chat about a recent high-profile natural disaster, the handler might comment on the unpredictability of life and the importance of leaving a legacy.
