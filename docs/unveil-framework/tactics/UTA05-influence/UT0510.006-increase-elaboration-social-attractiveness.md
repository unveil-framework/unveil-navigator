# UT0510.006 - Increase elaboration: Social attractiveness

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler can improve the source's perception of their social attractiveness to motivate information processing. Social attractiveness involves traits such as likability and similarity to the source. This technique may exploit the halo effect, where individuals who are attributed a positive quality are often attributed with a wide range of favorable traits.
