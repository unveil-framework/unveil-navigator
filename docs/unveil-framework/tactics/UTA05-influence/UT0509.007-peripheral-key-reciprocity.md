# UT0509.007 - Peripheral key: Reciprocity

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may invoke the principle of reciprocity to enhance persuasion. By providing favors, concessions, or gestures of goodwill to the source, the handler can trigger a sense of obligation or indebtedness. Leveraging reciprocity as a peripheral key encourages the source to reciprocate the handler's actions, increasing their receptiveness to persuasion.
