# UT0512.003 - Argument in itself: Physical attractiveness

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may leverage physical attractiveness to influence the source's perception and behavior. This involves presenting oneself in a manner that is visually appealing, such as dressing neatly, maintaining good grooming, and exhibiting confident body language. Physical attractiveness can subconsciously enhance the source's receptiveness to the handler's message and increase the likelihood of persuasion. Although what is considered physically attractive varies greatly among cultures and even individuals, this technique can be generated even by just looking polished and clean.
