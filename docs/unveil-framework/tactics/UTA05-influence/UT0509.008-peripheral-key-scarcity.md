# UT0509.008 - Peripheral key: Scarcity

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may leverage the principle of scarcity to enhance persuasion. By emphasizing the limited availability or exclusivity of an opportunity, product, or information, the handler can increase its perceived value and desirability. Leveraging scarcity as a peripheral key motivates the source to take action to secure scarce resources or opportunities, thereby enhancing their receptiveness to persuasion.
