# UT0509.004 - Peripheral key: Familiarity

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may capitalize on familiarity with the source to enhance persuasion. Establishing a sense of familiarity through repeated interactions, shared experiences, or mutual acquaintances can foster trust and rapport. By leveraging familiarity as a peripheral key, the handler can increase the source's receptiveness to their persuasive messages.
