# UT0501.002 - Tailor the message:Sensory filters (VAKO)

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to the source's most pervasive sensory filter. This technique is based on the VAKO filter, a classification of the most predominant sensorial register used by the source, which also may be the most easy to remember90. In this sense, the handler will primarily focus the content of their messages on one or more of the following contents: visual, auditive, kinesthetic and olfactory. This will, in turn, make the source feel more familiar and, therefore, comfortable with the handler. For example, if a handler wants a source whose predominant sensory filter is auditory, they could prefer to call the source or send audio messages, as well as talking about their music taste or sharing songs.
