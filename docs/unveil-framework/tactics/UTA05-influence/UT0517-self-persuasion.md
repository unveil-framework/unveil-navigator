# UT0517 - Self persuasion

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler can generate cognitive dissonances on the source seeking to influence their behavior in a way that benefits the operation, persuading the source by making them change their behavior. Although a cognitive dissonance can be generated in multiple ways, it is a process that can be easily generated by making the source alter their behavior, which will in turn make them involuntarily justify their actions by changing what they think. For example, making an individual argue in favor of something they initially did not agree with, or making them do an action they initially did not enjoy, would initiate a cognitive dissonance and, subsequently, increase the probability of changing their attitude towards an object.
