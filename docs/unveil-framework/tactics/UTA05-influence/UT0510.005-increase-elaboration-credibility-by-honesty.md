# UT0510.005 - Increase elaboration: Credibility by honesty

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may increase the source's perception of their honesty to enhance the source's elaboration. Honesty can be easily displayed by being coherent and impartial, for example, arguing not only in your favor but also against you. For example, a handler could tell a source that “they shouldn’t be really telling them about something” or that “what they are going to say could turn the source against them”, which showcases trust in the source.
