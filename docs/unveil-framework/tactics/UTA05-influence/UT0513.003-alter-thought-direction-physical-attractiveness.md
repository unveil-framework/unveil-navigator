# UT0513.003 - Alter thought direction: Physical attractiveness

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may capitalize on physical attractiveness as a persuasive factor by presenting themselves in a manner that enhances their appeal to the source. This could entail grooming, attire, body language, and other visual cues aimed at conveying attractiveness and fostering a favorable impression. Leveraging physical attractiveness can contribute to building initial rapport and predisposing the source towards receptiveness to the handler's overtures. Although what is considered physically attractive varies greatly among cultures and even individuals, this technique can be generated even by just looking polished and clean.
