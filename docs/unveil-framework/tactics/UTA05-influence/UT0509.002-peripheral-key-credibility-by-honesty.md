# UT0509.002 - Peripheral key: Credibility by honesty

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may bolster credibility by demonstrating honesty and sincerity in their communication with the source. Transparency and truthfulness can foster trust and credibility, making the source more receptive to the handler's persuasion attempts. Honesty can be easily displayed by being coherent and impartial, for example, arguing not only in your favor but also against you. In this regard, a handler could tell a source that “they shouldn’t be really telling them about something” or that “what they are going to say could turn the source against them”, which showcases trust in the source.
