# UT0509.011 - Peripheral key: Focus on benefits

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may emphasize potential gains or benefits to enhance persuasion. Highlighting the rewards, profits or advantages associated with complying with the handler's request can motivate the source to take action. Leveraging a focus on earnings as a peripheral key appeals to the source's self-interest and desire for personal gain, increasing their receptiveness to persuasion. This strategy taps into the source's motivation to maximize their own benefits and outcomes.
