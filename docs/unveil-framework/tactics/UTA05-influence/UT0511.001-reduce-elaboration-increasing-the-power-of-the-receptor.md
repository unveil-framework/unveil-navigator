# UT0511.001 - Reduce elaboration: Increasing the power of the receptor

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may strategically increase the perceived power or status of the receiver in order to reduce their elaboration. By making the receiver feel more authoritative or influential, they may be less inclined to critically evaluate the information being presented to them, thus reducing their elaboration level.
