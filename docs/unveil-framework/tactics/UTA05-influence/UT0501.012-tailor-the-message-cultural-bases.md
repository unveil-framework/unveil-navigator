# UT0501.012 - Tailor the message:Cultural bases

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may tailor their messages to reflect the cultural norms, values, and customs of the source's cultural background. By demonstrating cultural sensitivity and awareness, the handler can build trust and rapport with the source. If a handler is working to gather intelligence from a source in a foreign country, they might tailor their messages to align with the source's cultural norms and values. For example, if the source is from a culture that places high importance on respect and hierarchy, the handler would use formal language, honorifics and acknowledge the source's expertise or status in their communications. Additionally, the handler might reference local customs, holidays or historical events to show a deep understanding and respect for the source's background. This cultural sensitivity helps to build trust and rapport, making the source more likely to share valuable information.
