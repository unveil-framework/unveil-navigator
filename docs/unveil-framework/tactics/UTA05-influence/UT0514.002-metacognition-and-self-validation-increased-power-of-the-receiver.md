# UT0514.002 - Metacognition and self-validation: Increased power of the receiver

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may subtly convey messages or cues that highlight the source's power or authority in certain contexts. By acknowledging and affirming the source's status or influence, the handler aims to empower the source and reinforce their sense of self-worth. This can lead to increased trust in the source's own thoughts about the proposal presented by the handler.
