# UT0512.008 - Argument in itself: Fear

**Tactic:** [UTA05 - Influence](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may utilize fear by highlighting potential threats, risks, or negative outcomes associated with a decision or an action. This involves instilling a sense of urgency and concern in the source by emphasizing the consequences of inaction or unfavorable to the operation actions and the importance of taking proactive measures. However, in order for this technique to be correctly applied it's crucial to calibrate the level of fear appropriately, ensuring that it is neither too overwhelming nor too mild, to explicitly mention the negative, undesirable consequences of an unwanted action and to offer feasible solutions (or, at least, that the source considers feasible) and courses of action to alleviate the source's fears.
