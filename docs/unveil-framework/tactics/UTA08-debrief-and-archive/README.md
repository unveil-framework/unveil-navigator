# UTA08 - Debrief and Archive

## Description

The handler is trying to report all the information they obtained during the encounter or
interaction with a source, archiving it correctly. Although some of the information recovered
from human interactions is assumed to remain tacit, online handling has the advantage of the
information already being digitized to some extent. This is remarkable since while the
recording of a conversation in the real world can be considered an intrusive and aggressive
behavior, in online messaging apps it is assumed the “feature” of textual conversations are
being logged by both sides.
In this regard, it is paramount to pay attention and keep track of the way in which the
information is collected and stored. In many cases, this tactic will (and should) coexist with preexisting approaches of conventional real-life human handling.
