# UT0815.006 - Assess content’s accuracy: SOCINT

**Tactic:** [UTA08 - Debrief and Archive](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may verify the accuracy of the information with Socialmedia intelligence (SOCINT) sources.

The handler is trying to exit an interaction or to close the relationship with the source. This process requires careful planning to ensure the termination of contact does not provoke undesirable reactions or leave loose ends that could compromise the operation or the safety of either the handler or source.
