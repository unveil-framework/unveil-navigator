# UT0808 - Manual text transcription

**Tactic:** [UTA08 - Debrief and Archive](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may take on his own the transcription role to effectively register the content of a conversation previously recorded. Additional comments and notes can be added to the transcription to register emotions, feelings or side thoughts that might not be easy to identify in both the raw conversation or an automated transcription. Note that this effort might also take place after an automated transcription to highlight elements of the conversation which could not be processed properly due to environmental issues.
