# UT0903 - Openly terminate relationship

**Tactic:** [UTA09 - Exit](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may formally end the relationship with the source. This involves a definitive conclusion to their interactions, often needed by the completion of the source’s usefulness or potential security risks. The termination process should be handled with care to ensure that the source does not feel betrayed, which could lead to retaliation, feelings of revenge, or potential leaks.
