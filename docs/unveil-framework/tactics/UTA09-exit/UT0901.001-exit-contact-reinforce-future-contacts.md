# UT0901.001 - Exit contact: Reinforce future contacts

**Tactic:** [UTA09 - Exit](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may ensure that while the current interaction concludes without further immediate obligations, the source remains engaged and open to future contact. This technique involves reinforcing the importance of the relationship and expressing appreciation for the source's past cooperation. The handler may subtly emphasize the ongoing value the source could provide, hinting at future discussions without setting specific expectations, or maybe even hinting at possible future reinforcements. This approach helps maintain a positive rapport and keeps the communication channels open for re-engagement at a later date. This subtechnique should be planned and executed while considering the personality and motivational profile of the source, as individual's with a higher extraversion and neuroticism tend to be more vulnerable to rewards. Moreover, the type of reward, whether anticipated or given at the time of the exit, should also be tailored to the person. Therefore, this technique works best when combined with the behavioral techniques in UTA05 - Influence.
