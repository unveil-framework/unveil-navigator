# UT0901 - Exit contact

**Tactic:** [UTA09 - Exit](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may end a single contact session or interaction with clear closure, not wanting to close the door open for future interactions. This may include summarizing what was discussed, thanking the source for their cooperation, and subtly indicating when they might next be in touch. The difference with “UT0902 - Foresee future contacts'' is that the contact ends with no specific additional tasks for the source. Therefore, this technique can be used with both onetime contacts and continuous and formal contacts, whereas “UT0902 - Foresee future contacts” can not be used with one-time contacts, as in this kind of contacts the sources do not get tasked.
