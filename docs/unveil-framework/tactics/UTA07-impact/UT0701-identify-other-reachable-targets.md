# UT0701 - Identify other reachable targets

**Tactic:** [UTA07 - Impact](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may target the source as a way of identifying new possible sources that can be approached in the future. This involves analyzing the source’s professional and personal relationships, affiliations, and frequent interactions to map a network of individuals who might possess valuable information or influence. The handler looks for strategic entry points within this network, planning subsequent approaches based on the identified connections and their relevance to the operation’s goals.
