# UT0408.002 - Conceal identity: Safe communication protocols

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may employ safe communication protocols to conceal their identity and maintain operational security. This involves using encrypted messaging platforms (such as Bitmessage or Tox), anonymous email services or email services with no or low know your customer (KYC) policies, and other secure communication channels to exchange information with the source. By adhering to strict communication protocols and best practices, the handler can mitigate the risk of exposure and ensure that their true identity remains undisclosed.
