# UT0403 - Acquire profiles

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may acquire different social media or equivalent profiles in order to make the fake persona more real and/or to communicate with the source. This does not only reduce the need for enrichment (though it does not eliminate it), but also it may provide followers and relationships. The difference with “UT0402 - Create profiles” is that this technique implies the acquisition (potentially, paying for them) of profiles. The newly acquired profiles may represent a fake identity (a non-existent persona) or a third person unaware of the usage of their identity for this operation. However, impersonation entails legal consequences that the handler must take into account before carrying it out.
