# UT0404.001 - Compromise real profiles: Source

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may compromise the real profiles of the source, such as social media accounts, email accounts, or other online identities, in order to gain access to information directly related to the source. This may involve techniques such as phishing, spear phishing, or social engineering attacks targeted specifically at the source. By gaining control over the source's online presence, the handler can gather valuable intelligence and monitor the source's activities without their knowledge.
