# UT0402 - Create profiles

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may create different social media or equivalent profiles in order to make the fake persona more real and/or to communicate with the source. The content of the created profiles must be coherent with the contents of the identity and must be kept alive, that is, taken care of during the whole of the operation or as long as it is needed by interacting from time to time. The newly created profiles may be associated with a fake identity (a non-existent persona), the real handler (but with less details or altered information) or a third person unaware of the usage of their identity for this operation. However, impersonation entails legal consequences that the handler must take into account before carrying it out.
