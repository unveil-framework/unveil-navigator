# UT0401.006 - Create a false identity: Social identity and group affiliation

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may enrich a previously designed or a newly created identity by including their membership in groups. The handler may include information about the belonging and reference groups of the persona, which include: ethnic background, religion, gender, sex, nationality, sexual orientation and any other group of relevance to the operation.
