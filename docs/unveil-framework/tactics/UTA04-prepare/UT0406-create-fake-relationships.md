# UT0406 - Create fake relationships

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may enrich a previously designed or a newly created identity with relationships, whether real or fake. These relationships can be of different kinds and not just romantic or platonic. The created relationships may be with real or fake people. It must be noted that the fake relationships that are created from nowhere may need to be enriched as well, which can, in turn, require more resources.
