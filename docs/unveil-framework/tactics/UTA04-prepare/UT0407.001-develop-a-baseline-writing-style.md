# UT0407.001 - Develop a baseline: Writing style

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may carefully craft the writing style used in interactions with the source to develop a consistent baseline. This involves considering factors such as tone, vocabulary, grammar, and level of formality to ensure that the fake persona's communications remain consistent with the selected profile. By maintaining a consistent writing style, the handler can reinforce the credibility of the persona and minimize the risk of arousing suspicion from the source. When developing the writing style of the fake persona, the handler may consider the following factors and tailor them to the source:

Formality level. Determine the appropriate level of formality based on the nature of the relationship with the source and the context of the communication.

Tone. Choose a tone that aligns with the desired perception of the fake persona, whether it be friendly, professional, authoritative, submissive or casual.

Vocabulary. Select vocabulary that reflects the persona's background, education level, and cultural context, ensuring that it is consistent with the selected profile.

Grammar. Pay attention to grammatical conventions and writing standards to maintain coherence and clarity in the communication.

Sentence structure. Craft sentences that are easy to understand and in line with the persona's presumed linguistic capabilities and preferences.

Length of messages. Determine an appropriate length for messages to balance conveying necessary information with maintaining the reader's interest and attention.

Use of emoticons or emojis. Decide whether the use of emoticons or emojis is appropriate based on the persona's personality and the nature of the relationship with the source, as well as the source’s profile and behavior.

Frequency of greetings and farewells. Establish a consistent pattern for greetings and farewells to maintain continuity and familiarity in the interaction.

Consistency in formatting. Maintain consistency in formatting elements such as font style, size and color to create a cohesive visual impression.

Personalization. Incorporate personal touches or details into the communication that align with the persona's backstory or interests, enhancing authenticity and rapportbuilding with the source.
