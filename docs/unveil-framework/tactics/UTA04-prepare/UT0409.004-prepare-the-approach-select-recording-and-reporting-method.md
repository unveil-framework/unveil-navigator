# UT0409.004 - Prepare the approach: Select recording and reporting method

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify and select one or multiple recording and reporting methods that will be used during the operation. This involves choosing the most appropriate technology or method to record the interaction with the source. This could include audio recording, video recording or written notes. The choice depends on factors such as the sensitivity of the information being discussed, the preferences of the source and the resources available. If dealing with sensitive information or sources in potentially hostile environments, encryption and secure storage methods may be necessary to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the recorded data.
