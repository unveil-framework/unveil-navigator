# UT0407.002 - Develop a baseline: Posting times

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may establish a baseline for the posting times of the fake persona's interactions with the source. This way, the handler can establish a pattern that may give information about the job, habits or time zone of the fake persona. This technique also involves determining optimal times for communication based on the source's habits and preferences, as well as external factors such as time zone differences. By consistently posting at specific times, the handler can create a predictable pattern of interaction that reinforces the authenticity of the persona and facilitates the ongoing engagement with the source.
