# UT0407.005 - Develop a baseline: Adapt baseline

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may periodically review and adapt the established baseline to account for changes in circumstances or objectives. This involves monitoring the effectiveness of the baseline in achieving strategic goals and making adjustments as necessary to optimize performance. By remaining flexible and responsive to evolving dynamics, the handler can ensure that the fake persona's interactions with the source remain effective and aligned with operational objectives.
