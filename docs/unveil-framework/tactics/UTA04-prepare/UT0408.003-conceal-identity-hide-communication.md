# UT0408.003 - Conceal identity: Hide communication

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may find it necessary to hide the mere existence of a communication itself under certain circumstances, even when participants' identities and confidentiality are guaranteed. In such cases, handlers should identify cover channels and employ steganographic tools to conceal the messages being exchanged, including encrypted ones, to avoid detection. Steganography can be applied to a variety of cover channels, such as images, videos or even text. By embedding messages within these media types, handlers can ensure that the communication remains undetected by adversaries or monitoring systems, thereby enhancing the operational security of the HUMINT operation.
