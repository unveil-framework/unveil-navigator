# UT0401.004 - Create a false identity: Motivation profile

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may use a previously designed or a newly created identity with a specific motivational profile, that is, whether they are more easily influenced by rewards or punishments, as well as their reaction to fight or flight situations. The motivational profile of the fake persona depends on and correlates with their personality, so both should be taken into account together when designing a fake identity.

This subtechnique is based on Gray’s model83 84 85, which defines three motivational systems: the Behavioral Activation System (BAS), the Behavioral Inhibition System (BIS) and the FightFlight System. Handlers may identify if the source is more vulnerable to reinforcements due to having an overly activated BAS (typical in individuals with high neuroticism and extraversion) or if they are more vulnerable to punishments due to having a highly activated BIS (characteristic of introverted-neurotic people), as well as if their reaction to a dangerous situation would be to fight (such as low FFS and, therefore, high psychoticism individuals) or flight (more common in high FFS and low psychoticism individuals).
