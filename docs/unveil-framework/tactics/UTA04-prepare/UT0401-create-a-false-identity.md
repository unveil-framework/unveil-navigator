# UT0401 - Create a false identity

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may create a new identity in order to conceal their own, which will be endowed with as much details as possible or needed. A false identity or persona does not necessarily mean a whole new individual designed by the intelligence agency or HUMINT team, as anything other than the real identity of the handler would be considered a fake identity. In this sense, changing the surname of the handler or equivalent changes can be considered a new identity that must be cultivated accordingly, taking into account if the operation it will be used in will be elicitation or collaboration. It is specially relevant in this technique to make sure that the selected traits, behaviors, groups, etc. of the persona are coherent and tailored to the specific source and operation.
