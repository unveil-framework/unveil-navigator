# UT0404.003 - Compromise real profiles: Other third parties

**Tactic:** [UTA04 - Prepare](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may compromise the real profiles of other third parties who are not in the source’s standard circle. This could include compromising the profiles of individuals or entities that the source communicates with regularly, such as administrators of known platforms the source uses or any other individual or entity that may have visibility of the source’s profile. .
