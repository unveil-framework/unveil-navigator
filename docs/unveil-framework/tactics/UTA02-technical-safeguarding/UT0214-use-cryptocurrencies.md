# UT0214 - Use cryptocurrencies

**Tactic:** [UTA02 - Technical Safeguarding](./README.md)

## Description

This TTP involves the strategic use of cryptocurrencies to conduct financial transactions that are necessary for operational activities while maintaining a high degree of anonymity and operational security. Cryptocurrencies, due to their decentralized nature and potential for nontraceable transactions, serve as an effective tool in operations that require confidentiality and financial discretion.
