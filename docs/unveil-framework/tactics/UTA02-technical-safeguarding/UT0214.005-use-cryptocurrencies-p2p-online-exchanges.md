# UT0214.005 - Use cryptocurrencies: P2P online exchanges

**Tactic:** [UTA02 - Technical Safeguarding](./README.md)

## Description

Peer-to-peer (P2P) cryptocurrency online exchanges enable direct transactions between individuals without the need for an intermediary. This method allows the handler to buy, sell or trade cryptocurrencies directly with other users, often with minimal or no identity verification. P2P exchanges are advantageous for maintaining operational security and anonymity since they typically offer greater flexibility in terms of transaction terms and may support various payment methods. The handler can use P2P exchanges to acquire cryptocurrencies in a decentralized manner, making it harder for adversaries to track or block the financial resources necessary for the operation.
