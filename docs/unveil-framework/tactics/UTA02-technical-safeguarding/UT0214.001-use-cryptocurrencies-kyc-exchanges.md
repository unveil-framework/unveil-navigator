# UT0214.001 - Use cryptocurrencies: KYC exchanges

**Tactic:** [UTA02 - Technical Safeguarding](./README.md)

## Description

This technique involves using cryptocurrency exchanges that make use of Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures. These exchanges typically demand users to provide identification and other personal information to comply with regulatory requirements. Despite the loss of anonymity, using KYC exchanges may be strategically beneficial for handlers who need to access large amounts of cryptocurrency or interact with specific financial networks and can be considered an option depending on the resources of the adversary. Handlers may use these platforms when the operation requires access to a wide range of financial services or when the operational cover aligns with the legitimacy of such exchanges. However, handlers must carefully manage the identity and information provided during the KYC process to avoid compromising the operation.
