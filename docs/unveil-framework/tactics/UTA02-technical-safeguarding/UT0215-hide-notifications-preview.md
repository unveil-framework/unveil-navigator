# UT0215 - Hide notifications preview

**Tactic:** [UTA02 - Technical Safeguarding](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may configure all operational devices to hide notification previews on lock screens and notification centers. This measure ensures that sensitive information is not exposed inadvertently when devices are unattended or accessed by unauthorized individuals. By preventing the display of message content in notifications, the handler reduces the risk of accidental data leakage, maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of communication.

The handler is trying to obtain information about the possible sources in order to evaluate them and select those that could be a better match regarding the operation requirements. This tactic includes actions regarding the profiling of the sources and the identification of their baseline if possible, as well as the selection of one or more sources. Though in some manuals the concept of screening is exclusively used to refer to the process of identifying a source's access to the information and willingness to collaborate, in this model it also includes the profiling of a source, the identification of their baseline and the action of choosing an individual. Although the process of screening sources can be done in parallel with the collection of information, it is advised to be done previously in order to both it be done adequately and to subsequently tailor the strategy to the chosen source. Moreover, the screening process may be carried out by a professional other than the final handler, namely the screener or profiler, which will in turn subsequently pass the profile information onto the handler or the handler's team.
