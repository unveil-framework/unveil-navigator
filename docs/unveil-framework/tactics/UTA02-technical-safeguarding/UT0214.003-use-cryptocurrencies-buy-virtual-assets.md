# UT0214.003 - Use cryptocurrencies: Buy virtual assets

**Tactic:** [UTA02 - Technical Safeguarding](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may use cryptocurrencies to purchase virtual assets and gift cards that can be leveraged in operational activities or used to be transferred directly to sources as a payment. Virtual assets may include digital goods, online services, or virtual real estate, among others. Also, these assets can be instrumental in establishing and maintaining operational covers or facilitating communication and coordination in a covert manner. By using cryptocurrencies for such purchases, the handler ensures that these transactions are conducted with a level of anonymity that reduces the risk of exposure. The use of virtual assets in operations may also help in disguising the nature of financial flows, making it more difficult for adversaries to discern the true purpose of the transactions.
