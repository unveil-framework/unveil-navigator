# UT0107.002 - Assess adversary’s cybercapabilities: Resources

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may investigate the resources at the adversary’s disposal in the cyber domain, including their access to hacking tools, availability of skilled personnel, financial backing for cyberoperations, and their collaborations with other cyberentities. This assessment helps in understanding the breadth and depth of the adversary’s potential to mount sustained cyber operations.
