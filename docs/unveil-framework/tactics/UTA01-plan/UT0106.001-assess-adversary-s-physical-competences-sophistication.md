# UT0106.001 - Assess adversary’s physical competences: Sophistication

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler assesses the level of sophistication in the adversary’s operational tactics and techniques in real life scenarios, including counter surveillance capabilities. This includes evaluating the complexity and execution of their previous operations, their ability to carry out covert actions, and their use of advanced physical surveillance or sabotage methods.
