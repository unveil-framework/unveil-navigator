# UTA01 - Plan

## Description

The handler and their team are trying to plan the strategic and operative goals of the HUMINT
operation. This includes the assessment of the timeliness, resources and tools, amongst
others, that will be allocated for the operation.
In addition to these considerations, the planning phase also involves setting clear objectives
that are both measurable and achievable, aligning with the overall mission of the intelligence
agency. The team must also anticipate potential challenges and prepare contingency plans to
address these as the operation unfolds. This comprehensive planning ensures that all aspects
of the operation are thoughtfully considered and integrated, thereby enhancing the likelihood
of its success.
