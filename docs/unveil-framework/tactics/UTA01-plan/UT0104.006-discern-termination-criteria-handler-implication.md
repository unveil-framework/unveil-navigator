# UT0104.006 - Discern termination criteria: Handler implication

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may feel personally involved with a source beyond professional limits which can critically undermine the operation's integrity and its outcomes. This criterion for termination is vital to maintain operational security and ethical standards. It is important to anticipate when possible potential risks of personal implication as well as indicators that may be announcing these behaviors. This will require the immediate reevaluation, and potentially, the termination of the operation or a change in the handler if the handler becomes personally implicated with the source in a manner that could affect judgment or operational decisions.
