# UT0104.005 - Discern termination criteria: Changes in strategy

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may need to prolong over a long period of time a relationship until strategic changes justify that the long-term nature of the relationship no longer exists. This could be due to changes in geopolitical situations, agency priorities, or achievement of long-term intelligence goals, but also because of the specific personal environment of the source.

Regular reviews of strategic interests and priorities as well as source’s interests and worries are essential to determine the continued relevance of such operations in the short term.
