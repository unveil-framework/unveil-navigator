# UT0104.002 - Discern termination criteria: Useless source

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify that a source is no longer useful for the achievement of the intelligence requirements. A source may initially seem valuable but could turn out to be useless due to providing inaccurate, outdated or irrelevant information. It's crucial for handlers to identify shortcuts beforehand to assess the quality and relevance of the information provided by the source. Identifying potential ways of confirming the accuracy in the planning phase helps with the assessment on whether an operation should be terminated to redirect resources more effectively to other sources.
