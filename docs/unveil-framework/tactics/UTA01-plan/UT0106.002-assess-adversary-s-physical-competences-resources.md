# UT0106.002 - Assess adversary’s physical competences: Resources

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler examines the physical resources available to the adversary, such as manpower, geographical reach, access to critical infrastructures and logistical support, including, but not limited to, capabilities to conduct legal actions and to use state means to influence companies and organizations under the geographical location.
