# UT0103.010 - Develop strategy: Coordinate with other units

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may need to coordinate with other units or agents while planning the operation or during the course of it. Although the segmentation of units and information is key to intelligence agencies, there may be cases in which some of the information required by the HUMINT handler may already have been obtained by other units, or vice versa.
