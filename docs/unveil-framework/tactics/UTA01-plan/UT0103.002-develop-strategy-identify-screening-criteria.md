# UT0103.002 - Develop strategy: Identify screening criteria

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify specific screening criteria, that is, preliminary criteria to guide the screening process in order to identify the source type which will most likely have access to the information needed to complete the collection requirements and the general operational objectives. For instance, if the objective is to gather insider information about cybersecurity threats within a corporation, the handler would establish criteria such as targeting individuals with job titles like "IT Manager" or "Cybersecurity Analyst" on professional networking sites. They may also consider factors such as the individual's history of posting about relevant topics, their connections within the industry, and their participation in online forums or groups focused on cybersecurity.
