# UT0103.009 - Develop strategy: Identify operation limitations

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify the specific limitations of the operation. Thus, the handler and their team must understand the constraints associated with the operational environment, the capabilities of the intelligence collection methods and tools being used and the available resources. A proper identification of the restrictions that the handler may face during the operation is key to adapting the plan to the environment in order to ensure that the collection requirements are being met.
