# UT0107.001 - Assess adversary’s cybercapabilities: Sophistication

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may evaluate how advanced the adversary’s cyber operations are, considering their known exploits, the complexity of their attack vectors, and their proficiency in bypassing cybersecurity measures. This includes analyzing past cyber incidents attributed to the adversary and assessing their capability to orchestrate sophisticated, targeted cyberattacks.
