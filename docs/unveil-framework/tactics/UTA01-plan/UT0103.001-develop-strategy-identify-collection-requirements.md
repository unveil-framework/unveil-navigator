# UT0103.001 - Develop strategy: Identify collection requirements

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify specific informational needs that must be satisfied by the chosen source. This technique differs from UT0101 - Discern general intelligence requirements in the fact that UT0101 refers to the general needs of the operation (for example, to locate a specific individual person of interest), while UT0103.001 refers to a tactic level (using the same example as the previous one, the source needs to know the dates the person of interest is in the country or how often they visit). Collection requirements will depend on the time available.
