# UT0104.001 - Discern termination criteria: Uncooperative source

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may terminate an operation if the source becomes uncooperative. This could occur due to a change in the source's circumstances, a shift in their loyalty or motivation, or increased risk of exposure. Handlers should be able to recognize when continued efforts with an uncooperative source may lead to diminishing returns or compromise the operation’s security.
