# UT0105 - Identify potential adversaries

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may analyze and identify entities or individuals who might pose a threat to the operation’s success. This involves obtaining information on known adversaries and analyzing patterns that could indicate potential threats. This technique is needed for preemptive risk management, allowing the handler to prepare adequate countermeasures and strategic responses.
