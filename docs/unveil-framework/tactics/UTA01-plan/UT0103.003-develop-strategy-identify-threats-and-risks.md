# UT0103.003 - Develop strategy: Identify threats and risks

**Tactic:** [UTA01 - Plan](./README.md)

## Description

The handler may identify the threats and risks that conducting the operation may entail, including those affecting the handlers, the team, the agency and the country.

For instance, if the operation involves engaging with a suspected terrorist cell, the handler would evaluate the risks to themselves, their team, and the broader agency, including exposure of the operation, legal repercussions, and potential retaliation. They would assess the likelihood of being tracked or traced back to their real identities and plan accordingly to mitigate these risks, ensuring robust cyber security measures and employing anonymous communication tools to protect the operatives and their country’s interests.
